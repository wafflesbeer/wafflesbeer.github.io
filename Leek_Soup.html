Leek Soup

From: CookBook
Yields 4 servings

Ingredients:

  • 2 shallots sliced
  • 4 large leeks
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • ½ cup dry white wine
  • 1 Splash of white vinegar
  • 1 tsp coriander
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Cook the leeks in butter for 10 minutes until soft. Add salt and pepper and a little sugar. Add wine and cook until reduced in half, about 5 minutes. Add broth and stir. Add coriander. Cook another 10 minutes. Before serving, add splash of vinegar.
