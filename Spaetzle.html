Spaetzle

From: CookBook
Yields 2 servings

Ingredients:

  • ½ cup flour
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • 1 egg
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1 ½ cups shredded cabbage
  • ⅓ onion sliced
  • ½ cup diced bacon
  • ¼ cup shredded gruyere
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Add milk egg and flour to a bowl and mix. Batter should be slightly thicker than pancake batter.
  2. Place batter in a colander over boiling water. Using a spatula, work the batter through the holes into the boiling water. Drain the dumplings, drizzle with oil and set aside.
  3. Cook cabbage and onions in butter on high until slightly charred. Lower the heat and continue to cook a little. Set aside.
  4. Add dumplings to pan with some oil and cook until slightly crispy. Add the cabbage onions and bacon and mix. Add shredded cheese and mix until melted. Salt and pepper to taste.
