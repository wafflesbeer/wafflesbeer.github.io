Artichoke Bacon Tart

From: CookBook
Prep 0:45  |  Cook 0:45  |  Yields 6 servings

Ingredients:

  • 1 Tbsp Grapeseed oil
  • 7 oz bacon, julienned
  • ½ Yellow Onion, finely diced
  • 1 shallot, minced
  • 1 Tbsp garlic, minced
  • 7 oz Artichoke hearts, julienned
  • ¾ cup cream
  • 1 Juice of half a lemon
  • 1 frozen pie shell
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan
  • ½ cup grated Asiago

Instructions:

  1. 1. In a pan, caramelize the onions and shallot over low heat.
  2. 2. In a large saucepan or dutch oven, cook the bacon in the oil until almost cooked. Add the onion and shallot and stir.
  3. 3. Add the artichoke hearts and garlic, stir and cook for 3 minutes. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. 4. Add the cream and lemon juice, cook until simmering then lower heat and cook a bit longer stirring frequently.
  5. 5. Take off heat and let cool. Pour mixture into the pie shell.
  6. 6. Top with the grated cheese. Cook in a 350 degree oven for 20 minutes, then broil until browned.
