Garlic Lemon Pasta

From: CookBook
Yields 2 servings

Ingredients:

  • 1 box pasta of choice
  • 5 cloves garlic minced
  • 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1 teaspoon oregano
  • 3 Tbsp olive oil
  • 3 Tbsp butter
  • 1 Juice of 1-2 lemons

Instructions:

  1. Cook pasta al dente and drain
  2. In a saucepan heat the oil and butter on medium.
  3. When butter is melted, asd garlic. Cook 2 minutes.
  4. Add red pepper flakes and oregano. Cook 1 more minute.
  5. Add past back to pot with the butter sauce and toss to coat on low heat.
  6. Add lemon juice and salt/parmesan to taste. Serve.
