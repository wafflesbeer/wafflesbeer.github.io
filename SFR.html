Sweet Soy Sauce Fried Rice

From: CookBook
Yields 4 servings

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 Small onion chopped small
  • 2 carrots
  • 8 oz char siu
  • 3 eggs
  • 4 tbsp sweet soy sauce
  • 4 tsp soy sauce
  • 1 White pepper
  • ¾ tsp MSG

Instructions:

  1. Pre cook char siu and scramble eggs
  2. Sesame oil in wok. Heat up and add carrots and garlic.
  3. Add rice and mix. Add sauce mix and stir.
  4. Add meat and eggs and stir.
