Meat Ravioli Filling

From: CookBook
Prep 0:20  |  Cook 0:45  |  Yields 6 servings

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb + ground beef
  • ⅓ lb Italian sausage
  • 1 yellow onion, diced small
  • 4 celery stalks, diced small
  • 4 cloves garlic, finely minced
  • 300 grams frozen spinach pack
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan
  • ½ cup grated Romano
  • 1 cup bread crumbs
  • 1 Glass of chicken broth
  • 2 tsp oregano
  • 2 tsp marjoram
  • 2 tsp thyme
  • 1 tsp ground sage
  • 1 egg

Instructions:

  1. 1. Cook the celery and onion in a pot with some olive oil until translucent. Add garlic and cook one more minute.
  2. 2. Add the ground beef and Italian sausage and cook, breaking up, until browned.
  3. 3. Drain all fat.
  4. 4. Add spinach and spices. Stir and cook a few more minutes. Add salt. Make a little saltier than you might want. 5. Let mixture cool. Soak the bread crumbs in some of the broth for a few minutes.
  5. 6. Add mixture to food processor with grated cheese, and soaked bread crumbs. Add one egg. Pulse until combined.
