Pork Schnitzel

From: CookBook
Yields 2 servings

Ingredients:

  • 1 Pork loin butterflied and pounded thin
  • 1 Flour
  • 1 Panko bread crumbs
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 Vodka or other booze
  • 1 Vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Pulse the bread crumbs in a food processor for 2 minutes. Strain the bread crumbs through a fine mesh strainer.
  2. Heat up oil in a wok or pan (4 on our stove).
  3. Season pork with salt and pepper and sprinkle a little vodka over both sides. Gently dredge in flour, then in egg, then in bread crumbs. Add to oil.
  4. While frying shake the pan to keep the oil moving and flip pork over a few times. Should take 2 minutes. Place on paper towel when done.
