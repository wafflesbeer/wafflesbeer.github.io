Fresh Pasta Dough

From: CookBook
Prep 0:20  |  Cook 0:08  |  Yields 4 servings

Ingredients:

  • 1 About 2 cups of flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 egg yolks

Instructions:

  1. Make a mound with the flour with a well in the middle big enough for the eggs. Place eggs in the well.
  2. With a fork, mix the eggs incorporating some flour a little at a time. Keep mixing incorporating until very thick.
  3. Using a scraper, start folding more flour into the dough. Switch to using your hands to knead once it is close to the consistency of play dough.
  4. Knead until elastic incorporating flour only to keep it from being to sticky.
  5. Form into a ball and wrap tightly in Saran Wrap. Let sit at least 30 minutes.
