Spanish Saffron Chicken and Rice

From: CookBook
Prep 0:20  |  Cook 0:30  |  Yields 4 servings

Ingredients:

  • Pinch saffron
  • ¾ packet Saxon Goya
  • 1 bay leaf
  • ½ onion diced
  • 3 cloves garlic minced
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • ¼ cup white wine
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 cup white rice
  • 1 Packet of chicken breast or thighs
  • ¾ cup thawed peas

Instructions:

  1. Add saffron to wine and let sit until ready.
  2. Brown the chicken in a pan in oil. Set aside
  3. In the same pan, add onion. Cook until soft. Add garlic. Cook another minute.
  4. Add wine mixture and cook until almost reduced.
  5. Add Sazon Goya, bay leaf, and tomato paste and mix.
  6. Add back in the chicken, broth and rice. Mix only to combine. Cover and cook on low for about 20-30 minutes. Add peas last 5 minutes.
  7. For soccer at crank up heats high once done and watch closely.
