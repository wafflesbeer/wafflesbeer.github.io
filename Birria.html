Birria Tacos

From: CookBook
Yields 4 servings

Ingredients:

  • 1 Tacos
  • 2 ½ lb chuck roast plus one optional beef shank
  • 1 corn tortillas
  • 1 Oaxaca cheese
  • 1 diced white onion
  • 1 Cilantro
  • 1 Limes
  • 1 Consommé
  • 4 whole tomatoes, sliced in half roasted in oven
  • 5 cloves garlic roughly chopped
  • 1 white onion sliced
  • 5 dried ancho chiles
  • 5 dried New Mexico chiles
  • 5 dried cascabal chiles
  • ½ cup white vinegar
  • 1 Tbsp apple cider vinegar
  • 6 whole cloves
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp black peppercorn
  • 1 ½ Tsp Mexican oregano
  • 1 tsp marjoram
  • ½ Tsp cinnamon
  • 1 large container beef stock

Instructions:

  1. 1. Cut up the dried chiles into 1 inch pieces and deseed. Toast briefly in a pan then add boiling water and let it rehydrate, about 20 minutes. Drain some water not all and add to blender.
  2. 2. Slice onion and dice garlic. Caramelize the onions. Soften garlic. Add to blender.
  3. 3. Roast tomatoes in oven and add to blender.
  4. 4. Briefly toast the cloves, peppercorn, and cumin seed and grind in spice grinder. Add to blender. Add other herbs to blender.
  5. 5. Add vinegar and salt to blender to taste and blend everything until smooth.
  6. 6. Salt and pepper the meat and place in a large pot. Add the purée and cover all of the meat with it. Let marinate in the purée for 3-4 hours.
  7. 7. Add beef stock to meat/marinade mixture and stir until meat is covered in liquid. Heat oven to 350 and place the pot of meat inside Cook for 3-4 hours until meat is fork tender.
  8. 8. Separate meat from consommé. Shred the meat. Let consomme cool and remove some of the fat that forms on top.
  9. 9. Heat up a flat pan or plancha and place a small patty of the shredded meat for each bowl of consommé you will be serving until the bottom is crisp. Set aside.
  10. 10. Dip tortillas in consommé. Add beef and cheese and a scoop of consommé. Fold and heat on plancha until crisp, flipping as necessary.
  11. 11. Add crisped beef patties to each bowl of consommé and serve with tacos. Squeeze lime on top of tacos.
